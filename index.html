Who We Are

Born from Nature,
Built on Trust

Rishee Mineral Water began with a simple belief: every person deserves access to pure, natural water. Founded in 2010 in the heart of Madhya Pradesh, we source our water from deep underground springs in the Vindhya mountain ranges — one of India's most pristine natural reserves.

Our state-of-the-art bottling plant uses 12-stage purification with zero compromise on quality. From families to corporations, Rishee has been a trusted name in hydration for over a decade.
